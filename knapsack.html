Fractional Knapsack

About the algorithm:

Fractional knapsack is a type of knapsack problem where items can be divided into fractions, allowing a fractional amount of an item to be taken. The goal is to maximize the total value of items taken while not exceeding the capacity of the knapsack. Unlike 0/1 knapsack, where items cannot be divided, in fractional knapsack, fractions of items can be selected, providing more flexibility in item selection and maximizing the overall value-to-weight ratio.

Time Complexity:O(nlogn)
